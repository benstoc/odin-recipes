Baked Salmon

Description

A healthy baked salmon recipe that is easy to make, and will help you gain confidence in preparing salmon and other fish recipes.

This easy baked salmon recipe makes for a meal that you can dress up with side dishes to serve to company, or enjoy as a simple and quick dinner on a busy weeknight. We like to serve this salmon with veggies that can be cooked on the stove top.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1.5 teaspoons sweet paprika
  • 1.5 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper
  • 1 (2-pound) salmon fillet
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit wit ha rack in the center position
  2. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar, salt, paprika, garlic powder, and pepper.
  3. Place the salmon on a rimmed baking sheet, skin side down. Brush the salmon with olive oil, then rub it with the spice mixture until completely coated
  4. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes (depending on thickness), or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
